💬 Let's Practice! 句型練習
Pattern 1 — 句型一
"I am a ___. [sound]!"
學生一邊模仿動作，一邊說出句子！
Student："I am a dog. Woof, woof!" 🐶
Student："I am a cat. Meow, meow!" 🐱
Student："I am a pig. Oink, oink!" 🐷
Student："I am a cow. Moo, moo!" 🐮
Student："I am a duck. Quack, quack!" 🦆
Student："I am a sheep. Baa, baa!" 🐑
Pattern 2 — 句型二
"What animal is this?" → "It's a ___!"
外師播放叫聲問，學生搶答！
Teacher："What animal is this?"  
Students："It's a dog!" 🐶
Teacher："What animal is this?"  
Students："It's a duck!" 🦆
