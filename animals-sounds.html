💬 Sentence Pattern 1  句型一
Pattern
I am a ___. [sound]!
我是一隻＿＿＿。（動物叫聲）！
🐶 Dog: "I am a dog. Woof, woof!"
🐱 Cat: "I am a cat. Meow, meow!"
🦆 Duck: "I am a duck. Quack, quack!"
💬 Sentence Pattern 2  句型二
Pattern
"What animal is this?"
"It's a ___!"
「這是什麼動物？」  「牠是一隻＿＿＿！」
Teacher: "What animal is this?"
Students: "It's a dog!"
Teacher: "What animal is this?"
Students: "It's a duck!"
