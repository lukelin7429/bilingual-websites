Unit 12: The Future of Fangyuan's Egg Industry
單元十二：芳苑蛋雞產業的未來

🎯 Learning Objectives 學習目標

  • Evaluate current egg industry practices against SDG criteria用 SDG 標準評估目前的蛋雞業做法
  • Design a sustainable egg farm concept設計一個永續蛋雞農場概念
  • Present and defend their vision to an audience向聽眾呈現並捍衛自己的願景

📚 Lesson Timeline 課程時程（4 節）

1
SDG Audit of Current Fangyuan Farms 現況農場 SDG 稽核Score current practices against each SDG 對照每個 SDG 為現況打分
2
Case Studies: Sustainable Farms in Europe 案例研究：歐洲永續農場Netherlands, Denmark — what can we learn? 荷蘭、丹麥——我們能學到什麼？
3
Design Workshop: Farm of the Future 設計工作坊：未來農場Sketch, plan, and calculate costs 繪圖、規劃並計算成本
4
Final Pitch Presentation 最終簡報呈現Present to parents, farmers, and community 向家長、農民與社區呈現
